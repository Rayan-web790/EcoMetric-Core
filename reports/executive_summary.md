# Executive Summary - eCommerce Customer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview
This analysis dives into the customer behavior of a multi-category e-commerce store using event logs from October 2019. The goal was to identify growth opportunities through funnel optimization and customer segmentation.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Revenue**: $1,210,365.99
- **Total Users**: 399,664
- **AOV (Average Order Value)**: $41.27
- **Overall Conversion Rate**: ~7.3% (Purchase / Total Sessions)

---

## 🚀 Key Business Insights

### 1. Funnel Friction & Abandonment
**Finding**: There is a massive drop-off from **View to Cart**. Only ~4.5% of products viewed are added to the cart. However, once in the cart, the conversion to purchase is highly efficient.
- **Impact**: High traffic is being wasted at the top of the funnel.
- **Recommended Action**: Improve product page (PDP) engagement. Use "Recommended for You" carousels or limited-time offers to increase "Add to Cart" intent.

### 2. High-Value Segments (VIPs)
**Finding**: Customers in the **VIP / Champions** segment (high Recency, Frequency, and Monetary scores) contribute to over 40% of the total revenue despite being a minority of the user base.
- **Impact**: The business is highly dependent on a small group of power users.
- **Recommended Action**: Implement a VIP loyalty program. Prioritize these users for exclusive early-access sales and concierge-style support to maximize retention.

### 3. "At-Risk" Recovery
**Finding**: A significant portion of revenue comes from "Potential Loyalists" who haven't purchased in the last 15 days of the month.
- **Impact**: Without intervention, these users may churn to competitors.
- **Recommended Action**: Deploy personalized "We Miss You" email campaigns with a discount code triggered after 14 days of inactivity.

### 4. Peak Activity Windows
**Finding**: Activity peaks between **10 AM and 2 PM** local time, with a secondary surge in the evenings.
- **Impact**: Marketing spend (ads, emails) outside these windows has lower ROI.
- **Recommended Action**: Schedule promotional pushes and social media posts for 9:30 AM to catch users as they start their peak browsing period.
